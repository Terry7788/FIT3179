Olympics Visualization 2 by Terry Truong

The Summer Olympics has been one of the most significant sporting events globally, with an increasing number of participating countries over the years. This visualization shows the number of countries that participated in each Summer Olympics, starting from 1896 to 2021. The steady increase demonstrates the growing global inclusivity and popularity of the games. Explore the chart on the right to see how participation has evolved over time.

This chart visualizes the distribution of male and female athletes at the Summer Olympics from 1896 to 2024. It highlights the historical changes in gender participation, showing the growing number of female athletes over the years as the games have become more inclusive.
