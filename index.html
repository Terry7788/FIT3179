Olympics Visualization by Terry Truong

The Summer Olympics is one of the most significant sporting events globally, with an increasing number of participating countries over the years. This visualization shows the number of countries that participated in each Summer Olympics, from 1896 to 2021. The steady increase demonstrates the growing global inclusivity and popularity of the games. Explore the chart on the right to see how participation has evolved over time.

This chart visualizes the distribution of male and female athletes at the Summer Olympics from 1896 to 2024. It highlights the growing number of female athletes over the years as the games have become more inclusive.
