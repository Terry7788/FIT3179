What's the Purpose?

Every 4 years, athletes from across the world represent their countries in different sports in order to potentially earn a medal for their country. Earning a medal is an extremely prestigious title that many athletes can only dream of achieving. A medal signifies that the athlete are "one of the best" in the sport.

The Athletes

Many different types of athletes train countless hours and most their whole lives to be able to represent themselves and their country in their given sport. Initially in early years, mainly men competed in the olympics however in recent games, the percentage split between men and women have become more even, with the latest olympic games paris 2024 containing a 50:50 split between females and males.

Why Do We Have The Games?

Not only is the olympic games a great way to celebrate the achievements of athletes. It is also great in uniting the world together. However, due to the large viewership the olympic games obtains, this becomes a perfect opportunity for sponsors and brands to capitalize on. A big upside of the olympic games is the large revenue gained by televising the games.

Around 40% of the revenue source of the 2016 olympic games is contributed by the Local Sponsors.
